Welcome to PillPall

Your smart companion for tracking and managing your medications efficiently.

PillPall is a smart, easy-to-use Medicine Tracking App designed to help you stay organised and on top of your health. It keeps track of every medicine you take — from prescriptions to vitamins — showing details like dosage, schedule, and how much you have left. The app automatically reminds you when it’s time to take your next dose so you never miss a medication again, and it notifies you before any of your medicines expire, giving you time to safely replace them. With built-in AI assistance, PillPall can suggest optimal times to take your medicines, spot potential conflicts, and offer helpful insights based on your usage. It also connects to trusted sources like the HSE and other verified medical databases, ensuring you always have accurate, up-to-date information about your treatments and their uses.
